Local Time Google Calendar

View Any Public Google Calendar in Your Local Time Zone

Have you ever wanted to share a Google Calendar with people in different time zones without making everyone convert the event times themselves? This page makes that easy.

Simply provide the src value from any public Google Calendar embed link, and this viewer will automatically display the calendar using the visitor's own local time zone. Whether someone is in Arizona, New York, London, Tokyo, Sydney, or anywhere else in the world, Google Calendar will display the events in their local time.

This tool is perfect for:

  • Online events
  • Livestream schedules
  • Community calendars
  • Gaming events
  • Virtual conventions
  • Club meetings
  • International teams
  • Anyone sharing events across multiple time zones

No Google account is required to view the calendar, and visitors do not need to add the calendar to their own Google Calendar.

How to Use

Just copy and paste the src attribute from the embed link of your public Google Calendar after a ? in the URL like this:

?src=your_calendar@group.calendar.google.com

You can also provide an entire Google Calendar embed URL:

?src=https://calendar.google.com/calendar/embed?src=...

So the final link would be like this:

https://erallie.github.io/Local-Time-Google-Calendar/?src=your_calendar@group.calendar.google.com

or

https://erallie.github.io/Local-Time-Google-Calendar/?src=https://calendar.google.com/calendar/embed?src=...

When someone opens your generated link, the calendar is automatically displayed in their own local time zone, making it easy to share schedules with people anywhere in the world.
